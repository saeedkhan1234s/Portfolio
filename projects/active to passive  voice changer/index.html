Understanding Active and Passive Voice

In English grammar, active and passive voices are essential for sentence structure and clarity...

Active Voice: The subject performs the action (e.g., "The cat chased the mouse").

Passive Voice: The object of the action becomes the subject (e.g., "The mouse was chased by the cat").
